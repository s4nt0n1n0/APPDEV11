S.Y. 2024 — CNSC (1st Year) ▼

First Year Journey

CNSC: One of the 10 qualified transferees accepted into CNSC

I was initially unable to pass CNSC when I first took the exam, but I remained determined to study here due to our financial situation. Through perseverance, I became one of the ten qualifiers accepted via transfer.

Entering CNSC was intimidating. Coming from a small school, I struggled with insecurity, anxiety, and self-doubt during my first semester—especially without a proper laptop. Managing academic workloads was overwhelming, and I often felt inadequate.

During the second semester, I gradually became more comfortable. Although the lack of proper equipment continued to stress me until midterms, I was finally able to acquire a laptop afterward. With the shift to in-person classes, I completed my tasks more effectively and successfully passed all my major subjects.
